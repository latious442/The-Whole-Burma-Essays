Why parents should spend more time with their children

The tiny group already known as “Family” has long been existing from the Paleolithic age to current age with the tight, strong and true love strings. Although it is small when it is compared with other big national groups such as town and country, its countless warmness and kindness still stand uniquely. Family, the basic organization for growing youths who will make the country developed than previous, mainly consists of the gardeners’ called parents and the seedlings known as children. Actually, young children absolutely need the help of their parents apart from some exceptions of some orphans. So, if you will be a parent in the future and you want to be your children successful in life, you need to spend more time in the life- time of your children.
Frist of all, we were born with no ability except crying. So, we had to learn other major ability such as walking and speaking ourselves or with the help of others. In this time, on the other hands, when we are young, we count on our parents as if they were our heroes. As well as, when we get older guardedly, parents will occupy in almost all parts of our belief according to our opinion to our parents. So, for children, to be more confident in long-life passing goodly depends on our parents. From point of view of the parents, children are their lives and blood. They will do anything else for their children. However, there is a limit behind every greatest actions. Moreover, as today is a complex age with various kind of works. Parents must also work for their profession. For this reason, they will become spent less time with their time. Although it is a serious problem, there is a great effect on children. It is that children may feel anxiety, stress and doubt on their parent’s love. And then, when this activity happens continuously after day by day, the children will feel the taste of being lone, silent and difficult. This feeling is worse in almost children who are school-attended-age. In the teenagers, they will become uncontrollably in addiction of online games and drug due to the nature of teenagers. After a long time, they will be fall down under the influence of this annoying addiction. Sometimes, especially on family holiday, it seems it is just a moment but for those who are being stressed, this moment can bring the motivation to pass the next difficulties and can take the victory. Additionally, sharing our success with our parents in single time is a unique experience and it gives more strength to keep going. Actually, parents are likely to be your favorite book. They give effort whenever you want. They never decrease in motivating whenever and wherever. So, parents must spend their precious time with their children as long as they can if they want to be them convenience in finding the essence of their lives.
In the life which is full of dangerous thorns, having motivators or having parents sharing their time with you is completely same with occupying a greatest gift of the life. And, these gifts can only be given by kind parents. So, parents should share their time with their generations as if it were a greatest duty and responsibility of their whole life.
