Honesty is the best policy

Some people believe that it is best for people to be honest at all times. Others, however, believe that it is occasionally necessary to lie. Discuss both views and give your opinion. Our world is composed by various kinds of living thing including humans. People usually say that different people, different minds. It is true because our thinking and characters can’t be the same. So, that difference of mind is the reason of “How does the word OPPOSITE form”. If people thinking are the same, our world will be simple. As my point of view, the different minds become a lot of fields. And a lot of technologies that we mostly used today do not form if we have the same minds .By Phycology; people always want to do something new which is commonly different from basic form. Some people believe that it is the best for people to be honest at all times and some believe that it is quite true, however, it is occasionally necessary to lie. By seeing the first point of view, working with trust make us stronger than working with rules. Honesty is one of the types of mine that shows the human’s characteristic. Actually, honesty is the basic of the justice. It is important for everybody. So, ancient people say that to have the honest mind is more important than to have a beautiful body. The above sentence is acceptable. As we are Buddhist, honesty is not the strange with us. It is not easy not easy to try to be an honest people. It is the best for people to be honest. I will tell you one example to know how honesty is important. It is a test book of old G-11 namely “Yay Thay Pya Zat” This book indicate that how you are poor, if you have an honest mine, you can be rich. And unhonest can be danger. For the people who are ever honest, even they got a problem; there is a support that helps them because of their honest solving.
Although, honesty is important, sometimes we have to relinquish because of a serious condition. Owing to the bad mind of people and injustice world, sometimes, we must use the lie if it is necessary. Actually, real life is more difficult than you think. You will know that the honesty is not appropriate foe everybody when you have met various types of people. This is the key but nothing is good if it is over the limit. Second sentence is also reasonable. As my point of view, I think that we have to lie when it is occasionally necessary.
